Our Services?

Local Deliveries

• Standard Delivery (Same-Day): Reliable, cost-effective same-day delivery for businesses with urgent needs.

FAQ's

What services does Vrinda Logistic Solutions Limited offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of logistics services, including same-day local deliveries, next-day regional deliveries, urgent deliveries, and specialized services like fragile item handling, oversized parcel delivery, after-hours delivery, and removals. Our fleet is equipped to handle everything from small packages to large, bulky items.

How do I request a quote for a delivery?

You can request a quote by filling out our online quoting form available on our website. Simply provide details about your delivery, and our team will respond with a customized quote tailored to your specific needs.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve local and regional areas within a 100-mile radius. This includes city- wide deliveries as well as longer-distance regional shipments. If you require services beyond this range, please contact us to discuss your specific needs.

How quickly can you deliver my package?

We offer several delivery options based on urgency:
• Standard Delivery: Same day within local areas.
• Express Delivery: Within 4 hours for local deliveries.
• Urgent Delivery: Within 2 hours locally or within 6 hours regionally. We also offer next- day and same-day services for regional deliveries.

How do you ensure the safety of fragile or high-value items?

We take the utmost care in handling fragile or high-value items. Our team is trained in specialized packaging and handling techniques, and we use secure, padded transport vehicles to ensure your items arrive in perfect condition.

Can I track my delivery in real-time?

Yes, all deliveries can be tracked in real-time. Once your package is on its way, you'll receive a tracking link that allows you to monitor its progress from pickup to delivery.

What happens if there is a problem with my delivery?

In the rare event of a delivery issue, our customer support team is available 24/7 to assist you. We are committed to resolving any problems quickly and efficiently to ensure your satisfaction.

Do you offer discounts for bulk shipments?

Yes, we offer competitive discounts for bulk or regular shipments. Please contact our sales team for more details and to discuss a pricing plan tailored to your needs.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team 24/7 via phone at [0742001861] or email at [vrinda.logisticsolutions@gmail.com]. We’re here to help with any inquiries or issues you may have.

Do you offer after-hours or weekend delivery services?

Yes, we understand that business needs can extend beyond regular hours. We offer after-hours and weekend delivery services to ensure your logistics requirements are met whenever you need them.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a variety of payment methods including bank transfers, credit/debit cards, and online payment platforms. For regular clients, we also offer invoicing options. Please contact our billing department for more information.

Can I schedule a delivery in advance?

Absolutely. We encourage clients to schedule deliveries in advance, especially for large shipments or specific time-sensitive deliveries. Contact us to arrange a date and time that suits your needs.
